## Login

You know, just login, [privacy.apple.com](https://privacy.apple.com).

## Request Data Downloading

![The first option after login is to request downloading, just click the highlighted link.](https://s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/static.somarl.com/c9fc21c7-e887-4973-9ad9-5dd0ec564d20/request-downloading.jpeg)

![Click the highlighted link. You may also read more about request guides.](https://s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/static.somarl.com/e44c72f1-4177-4542-bda6-7b744f5bb91c/request-downloading-2.jpeg)

![Select the first row Apple Media Services information](https://s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/static.somarl.com/0c011fa9-11ca-4748-8f12-4dcf92d45508/select-media-information.png)

Since this is all we need, scroll to the bottom of the page and click "Continue".

![Click "Complete request"](https://s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/static.somarl.com/a4589690-22fc-49a5-a7ee-8e4f9dcf2c1f/choose-file-size.png)

## Complete and Wait

Since then the request is done, we have to wait for Apple to send the data to our mailbox.

It takes days, anyway, we have to wait.

![Since then the request is done, we have to wait for Apple to send the data to our mailbox.](https://s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/static.somarl.com/046e02b2-67d5-421b-9d3b-24edc265f119/complete-and-wait.jpeg)

![Back to the first page we visited, we may notice that a data request in progress appears at the sidebar.](https://s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/static.somarl.com/d920a0a4-d37d-487a-8650-8f914a49273e/request-in-progress.png)

## Download the Data

Time flies, finally Apple sent the email. We could download it now.

![Download the data](https://s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/static.somarl.com/b2e07439-04e9-43f9-ad88-ee258a615ac7/download-data.png)

Unzip the file, and you may find it.
